Romania

Recommendation for entertainment around Bran Castle! Mar 7, 2024; Username: ExpatInEurope

You cannot miss this!

The Castle of Horror, in the location of Bran Castle, has been one of the best experiences I’ve ever had in terms of an interactive experience. The atmosphere feels spooky from the moment you step in, and it gets progressively “worse” (truly better for my horror genre enjoyers), from Dracula popping from the ceiling to being followed on the stairs by vampires. I had truly the experience of my life. A downside would be that my pup, Robi, a 3kg Bichon was a bit scared of the whole setting, but he was the best companion. Overall, the price of 40 roni (7,5 euros) was worth it. Highly recommend checking it out!

Trusalova Camping: B E A U T I F U L

For the past week I’ve been staying at Camping Trusalova, in the region of Turiec, Slovakia, and I’m for sure going to stay a month more. To say that I am obsessed is an understatement. This region of Slovakia truly brings out what was mother earth has best to offer. The camping itself is spacious, well equipped with modern, and clean bathrooms, but it also has little cabins for those who would like to take some nights away from the bed of your campervan. I will give it a 9 out of 10, highly recommend if you want to stop somewhere in Slovakia.
