Who we are

Vandromeda is here to connect people on various places throughout Europe to fight the road-loneliness.

Vandromeda started as project of a small group of girls that wanted to help people on their journeys. We know it can be scary to start something new.

Travelers can meet each other at campsites by using our platform. Don't be afraid and check us out!

Our vision

In a world where travelling is within reach, we believe that you don't need to do it alone because only then we can create a community tied together with shared memories.
